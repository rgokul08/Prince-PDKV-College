About Our Institution

Established in 2009 by Prince Educational Society, PDKVCET has grown into a premier engineering institution spanning 65 acres in Chennai. Affiliated to Anna University (TNEA Code: 4116) and approved by AICTE, we hold NAAC A+ accreditation valid until 2029 and ISO 9001:2015 certification.

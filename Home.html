About PDKV College

Established in 2009 by Prince Educational Society, Prince Dr K Vasudevan College of Engineering and Technology is a premier engineering institution affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE.

Located in the serene Ponmar area of Chennai, our sprawling eco-friendly campus provides the perfect environment for academic excellence and holistic development.

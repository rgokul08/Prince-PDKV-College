About Our Institution

Established in 2009 by Prince Educational Society, PDKVCET has grown into one of Chennai's premier engineering institutions. Spread across 65 acres at Ponmar, our state-of-the-art campus features world-class laboratories, digital libraries, and modern hostels.

With NAAC A+ accreditation valid until 2029 and Anna University affiliation (TNEA Code: 4116), we offer 8 UG & 4 PG programs. Our 200+ faculty members bring decades of industry experience to nurture future engineering leaders.

Strategically located on Medavakkam-Mambakkam Main Road, we're just 5 minutes from Sholinganallur IT Park and 20 minutes from Chennai International Airport, providing unmatched industry connectivity.
